YOUR ADVANTAGES

TrainElite is the all-in-one place to track your daily workouts and nutrition. Track your workouts easily to stay consistent with your training! Log all your nutrition to stay on track and see the results you want!

Workouts

You can create and manage flexible workout routines for any goals you have. Select exactly which exercises you'll do and how many repetitions, time or distance you want to do them. You can also combine different workouts into a schedule.

Nutrition

Fitness starts with what you eat on a daily basis. Create your personal diet plan by creating as many meals with as many different ingredients as you need. The application will calculate the nutritional values (total energy, protein, carbohydrates, and so on) about the whole plan as well as the indivual meals.

Diary

Enter the weights and repetitions you did for each exercise to generate diagrams that let you see at a glance how well you are doing. The raw numbers are of course still accessible. You can also enter notes and a general impression (great, neutral, etc.)
